About Lit Mag

Literary Magazine is St. John's College High School's premier literary magazine (idk rephrase??), dedicated to showcasing the creative writing and artwork of our talented student body.

Each year, our student editorial board selects the finest poetry and artwork submitted by their peers for publication in our annual edition.

Lit Mag provides a platform for student expression and fosters a vibrant literary community within our school.

Submit Your Work

We're always looking for fresh voices and perspectives. If you're a St. John's student, we encourage you to submit your poetry, short stories, essays, photography, or artwork for consideration in our next edition.

Submission Guidelines

  • Written work must be original and previously unpublished
  • Poetry: Maximum 3 poems (40 lines each)
  • Word Count: Maximum 2,500 words
  • Artwork: High-resolution digital images (300 DPI minimum)

Submission Deadline

For the 2026 Edition: May 5th, 2026
